About

Built by oilfield people, for oilfield water.

IP Filtration solves one of the most expensive, least glamorous problems in produced water handling: solids. We remove them before they plug your wells, eat your pumps, and fill your tanks.

Produced water is getting dirtier and the volumes keep climbing. Across the Permian Basin and beyond, operators are moving more water through SWD wells, recycle facilities, and reuse loops than ever — and the solids that come with it are the silent driver of cleanouts, acid jobs, pump failures, and lost injectivity.

We focus on removing those solids upstream, automatically, so the rest of your system runs the way it should. Our flagship Automated Self-Cleaning Strainer does it with rotating brushes and no consumables. Where depth filtration is the better fit, we supply high-flow vessels and Coleman Filter Company's Torpedo, Bullet, Rocket, and string-wound elements — manufactured right here in Texas.

We'd rather prove it than pitch it. That's why nearly every relationship starts with a trial: we put a unit on your worst water and let the results make the case.
