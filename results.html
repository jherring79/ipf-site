Field test

Two units in series on skimmed AST material.

Two strainers were plumbed in series — a 300-micron basket followed by a 50-micron basket — to step down solids loading. The system was run on material skimmed off the top of an aboveground storage tank: some of the toughest fluid a strainer can see.

It performed exceptionally well. The setup removed all solidified paraffins, broke the emulsions, and filtered down to 50 microns — significantly improving fluid quality and proving the configuration for the front end of an SWD.
